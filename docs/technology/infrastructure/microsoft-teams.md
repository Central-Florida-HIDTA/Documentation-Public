# Microsoft Teams

## Overview

This document is a template for documenting Teams governance, collaboration controls, and telephony operations.

## Governance Baseline

- Team and channel creation policy
- Naming conventions and lifecycle policies
- Guest/external access model
- App governance and approval controls

## Collaboration Controls

- Information barriers and sensitivity labels
- File sharing policies
- Retention and legal hold behavior
- Meeting and recording controls

## Teams Phone (Template)

Document architecture and controls at a category level in public docs:

- Calling plans/direct routing model
- Auto attendants and call queues
- Resource account governance
- Number assignment and lifecycle process

Store detailed number inventories and account mappings in internal repositories.

## Security and Compliance

- MFA and session controls for Teams access
- DLP coverage for chat/channel and files
- Audit and monitoring coverage
- Incident response runbook references

## Operational Validation Checklist

1. Validate external/guest access settings
2. Validate app permission restrictions
3. Validate recording retention and compliance
4. Validate phone system routing and failover
5. Validate audit telemetry and alerting
